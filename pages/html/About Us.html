A group of Ndejje Senior Secondary School students are working on a project to raise awareness about the increasing issue of electronic waste. Their initiative is not just academic, but a proactive step towards addressing environmental concerns. They aim to educate others about sustainable e-waste management practices and contribute to a global movement advocating for recycling, proper disposal methods, and reduced electronic consumption. Their project demonstrates the power of youth engagement in environmental advocacy and their commitment to creating a cleaner, healthier planet for future generations.
